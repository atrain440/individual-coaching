Individual Pattern Shift Coaching — offering overview

Individual coaching offering — concept overview

Who this is for: Practitioners, change agents, and culture-aware people who are inside an organization's culture but don't have top-down buy-in to change it — and who want to shift their own patterns first.
Entry point

Pattern Snapshot Session — 60 min

Orientation to your personal patterns via the Pattern Shift Wheel. Identify 1–2 root patterns showing up in your work life right now.

Wheel walkthrough + personal pattern mapping
Unlearning Self-Assessment intro (USAM)
One "notice and name" practice to take away
Core offering

Pattern Shift Coaching — 90-day container

A structured coaching engagement built around one pattern cluster. Move through the Unlearning Cycle as a personal practice — Notice, Name, Challenge, Choose, Practice.

Monthly 60-min coaching sessions (3 total)
Pattern Shift Wheel diagnostic + debrief
Unlearning Cycle as personal practice map
Async support between sessions
Personalized pattern shift plan at close
For peer groups

Pattern Shift Circle — 4–6 people, 6 weeks

A cohort model for people navigating similar cultures together. Peer accountability + shared pattern recognition. Especially relevant for teams without org buy-in — or for co-founders doing culture-building from scratch.

Weekly 75-min facilitated sessions
Shared Pattern Shift Wheel orientation
Individual + group pattern mapping
Group compact at close
